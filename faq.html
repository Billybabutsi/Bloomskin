Find answers to common questions about our products and policies.

What is Bakuchiol and how does it work?

Bakuchiol is a plant-derived ingredient that provides retinol-like benefits without irritation.

How should I use the BloomSkin serum?

Apply 2-3 drops to clean skin morning/night. Follow with moisturizer and sunscreen.

When will I see results?

2-4 weeks for texture improvement, 8-12 weeks for visible reduction in fine lines.
